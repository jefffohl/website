About Jeff

Hi. I'm Jeff, and I try to make it easier for humans to use computers.

What is this page all about?

What you see happening on the right here started out as a doodle. Initially inspired by abstract works by artists such as Richard Diebenkorn, I have long been interested in how our minds interpret basic shapes. When we see an abstraction our minds invent an story or image, and we will fill in the gaps and try to match what we see with something we have seen before.
